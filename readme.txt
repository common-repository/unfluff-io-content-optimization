== Description ==
  
**Tired of low-quality content? Unfluff it.**
Quality content that keeps your readership engaged needs to do more than just reach a certain word count.
Unfluff helps keep your content on-point and easily readable, can detect AI-written content, and keeps your content team accountable.
And, it's 100% free.
  
== Installation ==
  
1. Upload the plugin folder to your /wp-content/plugins/ folder
2. Go to the **Plugins** page and activate the plugin
3. Go to the plugin settings page and add your license key

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I use this plugin? =
Past your content in the editor and click Unfluff check. Based on the length of the content, the plugin will take a few or more seconds to show you the result. 
  
= How do i get a license key =
Visit [Unfluff.io](https://unfluff.io) to get a free license key.
